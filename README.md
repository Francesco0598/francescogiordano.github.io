# Francesco Giordano

Welcome!

I am a PhD Candidate at HEC Paris. I am generally interested in topics of games and decision theory, with particular emphasis on aspects related to information and learning, and in applications related to entrepreneurship and startups. 

My research interests include:
- Game Theory
- Mechanism Design and Information Design
- Cognitive uncertainty
- Selection and mentorship

Other:
- I am collaborating with the ION Management Science Lab on topics related to selection and mentorship of startups 
- I am alumnus of QTEM and since my graduation I have been often coach for the QTEM Data Challenge 

You can learn more about my work, publications, and projects on this website.
